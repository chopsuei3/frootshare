Find local fruit
around your neighborhood

Find nearby fruit shared by others for a quick, healthy, and all-natural snack. Locations are added by people like you and shared with others via simple map interface. Locations should be accessible from the street and to the general public, and property owners are encouraged to place fruit near the street to avoid trespassing.

Share your extra fruit harvest

Do you have a fruit tree or shrub that produces more fruit than you need or want? Save yourself the hassle of cleaning up all that extra fruit that falls to the ground from not being picked. Simply share your bountiful harvest with the community and let them pick it for you so it doesn't go to waste.
